# RedPDF Refund Policy

*Effective 2026-07-16. Purchases are processed by Stripe; refunds are issued through the
original payment method.*

## 30-day money-back guarantee

If RedPDF doesn't work out for you, request a refund within **30 days of purchase** and we
will refund the full license fee — no justification required, though we'd love to hear what
fell short.

## How to request

E-mail the order reference to the support address on your receipt (or open a refund request
through the checkout provider). Refunds are processed to the original payment method,
normally within 5–10 business days.

## What happens to the license

Upon refund, the license terminates (see the License Agreement, § Termination). The license
key must no longer be used, and applications distributed with licensed features enabled must
be rebuilt without them or re-licensed.

## Exclusions

- Renewal periods already used: subscription renewals are refundable within 30 days of the
  renewal charge, pro-rated at our discretion.
- Purchases older than 30 days.
- Support-time already consumed under a priority-support agreement may be deducted.

## Trials

Prefer trying before buying — trial keys exercise every licensed feature. A refund request
that could have been a trial is still honored; we'd just rather save you the round trip.
